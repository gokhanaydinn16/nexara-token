Long Description:
Nexara (NXR) is a deflationary ERC-20 utility token that tokenizes access to Superajan, an AI-powered algorithmic trading bot on Binance USD-M perpetual futures. Token holders stake NXR to unlock tiered bot access (Bronze/Silver/Gold) and earn a proportional share of the bot's trading revenue. Every transfer permanently burns 2% of NXR, creating deflationary supply dynamics. The protocol features 12% APY staking rewards funded by transfer fees, anti-whale protection limiting wallets to 1% of total supply, and a 30-day staking lock period.
