[3] COIN DESCRIPTION (English)
Nexara (NXR) is a deflationary ERC-20 token that tokenizes access
to Superajan, an AI-powered algorithmic trading bot operating on
Binance USD-M perpetual futures markets. Token holders stake NXR
to unlock tiered bot access (Bronze/Silver/Gold) and earn a share
of the bot's trading revenue. Every transfer burns 2% of NXR
permanently, creating a deflationary supply mechanism. The protocol
features a 12% APY staking system funded by transfer fees, and
anti-whale protection limiting single wallet holdings to 1% of
total supply.

[4] COIN DESCRIPTION (Türkçe — kendi notun için)
Nexara (NXR), yapay zeka destekli Superajan trading botuna
stake ederek erişimi tokenize eden deflasyoner bir ERC-20
tokenıdır. Her transferde %2 yakılır, stake edenlere %12 APY
sağlanır ve bot gelirleri NXR stake edenlere dağıtılır.
