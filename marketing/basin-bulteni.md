## Nexara Launches NXR: A Deflationary Token That Tokenizes Access to AI-Powered Crypto Trading

**Nexara introduces a new model where users stake tokens to access an institutional-grade trading bot and share in its revenue — no subscriptions, no gatekeepers.**

---

Nexara has officially launched **NXR**, a deflationary ERC-20 token built on Ethereum that gives holders access to **Superajan**, an AI-powered algorithmic trading bot operating on Binance USD-M perpetual futures markets.

Unlike traditional trading bot services that charge subscription fees and keep all profits, Nexara introduces a **stake-to-access** model. Users stake NXR tokens to unlock tiered bot access — Bronze, Silver, and Gold — and earn a proportional share of the bot's trading revenue.

"Institutional trading has always been a walled garden," said **Gökhan Aydın**, Founder and Lead Architect of Nexara. "We built Nexara to tear down that wall. Every individual deserves access to the same intelligence the big players use — and deserves to share in the value it creates."

### Key Features

- **Stake-to-Access:** Three tiers unlock progressively more bot features and trading signals.
- **Revenue Sharing:** A portion of bot profits flows back to NXR stakers.
- **Deflationary Mechanism:** 2% of every transfer is permanently burned, reducing supply over time.
- **Staking Rewards:** 12% APY funded by transfer fees.
- **Anti-Whale Protection:** No single wallet can hold more than 1% of supply.

### Nexara Pay

Alongside the token, Nexara has launched **Nexara Pay**, a crypto payment gateway that allows merchants to accept ETH, NXR, and other cryptocurrencies directly — with card payments supported through licensed, regulated partners. Customers who pay with NXR receive an automatic discount.

### Technical Foundation

All Nexara smart contracts are open-source and verifiable on Etherscan. The protocol is built using audited OpenZeppelin libraries with reentrancy protection and transparent, no-hidden-mint tokenomics.

### Roadmap

Nexara's roadmap includes mainnet deployment, decentralized exchange liquidity, listings on CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap, on-chain governance, and multi-exchange support throughout 2026 and 2027.

### About Nexara

Nexara is a DeFi protocol founded by Gökhan Aydın, focused on democratizing access to professional-grade trading technology through tokenization. The project combines a working AI trading engine (Superajan) with a community-owned token economy.
