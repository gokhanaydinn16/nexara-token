One-Liner

Nexara (NXR) is a deflationary ERC-20 token that tokenizes access to Superajan, an AI-powered trading bot on Binance USD-M perpetual futures — stake to access, earn revenue share, 2% burn on every transfer.

Boilerplate (50 words)

Nexara is a DeFi protocol founded by Gökhan Aydın that democratizes access to professional-grade AI trading. Users stake NXR tokens to unlock tiered access to the Superajan trading bot and earn a share of its revenue. With a 2% transfer burn, 12% APY staking, and anti-whale protection, NXR combines real utility with sustainable tokenomics.
